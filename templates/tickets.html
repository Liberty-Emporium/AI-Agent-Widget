What is this page?

Tickets are conversations the AI couldn't resolve and escalated to a human. Every ticket is a customer waiting — respond fast.

  • In Progress — you've started the conversation
  • Resolved — issue handled, customer satisfied

Turning Tickets Into Sales

  • Ticket subjects reveal your agent's blind spots — add those answers to Knowledge Base
  • Many tickets are buying-intent questions — treat them as warm leads
  • A fast, helpful reply often converts into a paying customer
