Common Questions

Do I need my own API key?
Yes — you bring your own OpenRouter API key. This means you control your AI costs directly and we never markup your usage.
Can I switch plans anytime?
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately.
What happens if I exceed my message limit?
Your agent will pause until the next billing cycle. Upgrade anytime to keep it running without interruption.
Is my API key secure?
Yes. Your key is encrypted at rest and never exposed to end users. It's only used server-side to call OpenRouter on your behalf.
Can I embed on multiple websites?
Yes, on any paid plan. You can control which origins are allowed per agent using our CORS origin control.
